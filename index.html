Elemental Shooters

In this small 2-player platform shooting game, players strive to defeat each other by utilizing elemental damage relationships and effects

Fire counters Grass, Grass counters Water, Water counters Fire

Fire doubles player base damage, Water will increase bullet velocity and slow enemy, Grass will root enemy and heal self
